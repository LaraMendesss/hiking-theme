Hiking is a long, vigorous walk, usually on trails or footpaths in the countryside. Walking for pleasure developed in Europe during the eighteenth century. In the early days it was maybe just a walk, but now a days it is a proper sport where a lot of preparation and equipment is mandatory.

Nowadays hiking can go from one walk through the park to climb the mount Everest! The sky is the limit!
